AUGUSTO

I am a developer and designer working at the intersection of tech, art, design and community.

I spend a lot of time collaborating with artists to explore future modes of storytelling. I am the creator of DEAD ALIVE MAGAZINE.

I have worked as a software engineer since 2013--more about my work here.


I've tried switching to tea but espresso will always have my heart.

I used to split my time between creative writing and coding. These days I combine the two. I keep a blog of electronic literature techniques HERE.


Currently pursuing an MFA in Studio Art at Maryland College Institute of Art. I get my hands dirty with printmaking, graphic design and public art.


I am a Columbia University alum, where I studied computer science with a focus on natural language processing and literature.


For coding I prefer upbeat dance music. For creative writing I find silence is best.
